MOGGILL - Brisbane River Flood Forecast Report
This Brisbane River Flood Forecasting Report is intended to provide interested parties with property-specific forecast flood levels during a
Brisbane River flood event.
Disclaimer
This report is based on information issued by the Bureau of Meteorology at 28/01/2013 10:30:00 AM and relates to flooding from the
Brisbane River only.
This is the maximum flood expected at your property
The report does not contain information relating to flooding from creeks, storm tides, king tides, overland flow or local drainage.
This report only provides information in relation to properties that the forecasting software indicates may be at risk based on available external data.
Properties that are not listed may also be at risk and if your property is not listed you must rely on your own observations and analysis as to whether
your property may be at risk of Brisbane River flood or not.
How to read this report
1. Find your street, listed alphabetically in the table
2. Locate your house number
3. Read your `Land Flood Status' and refer to the definitions below
4. Read the flood height measurements at the lowest and highest part of your land
5. Determine the flood height on your land
6. Take approriate action
If your property is not listed:
- your property may be known by another address
- your property may be situated outside the flood cell for river flooding
Definitions
Likely to flood
­ based on the latest estimate of expected flood levels from the Bureau of Meteorology, part or all of the subject property is expected
to be affected by flood waters.
Unlikely to flood­ based on the latest estimate of expected flood levels from the Bureau of Meteorology, no part of the subject property is expected to
be affected by flood waters before the time given in this report.

Please see the Frequently Asked Questions for further information.
